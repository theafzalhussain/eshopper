📐 Design Guidelines

✅ Do's

✓ Maintain Aspect Ratio

Never stretch or distort the logo shape

✓ Use OG Colors

Always use metallic gold on midnight black

✓ Minimum Sizes

Horizontal: 100px width minimum

✓ Clear Space

Keep 20% clear space around logo

✓ Vector Format

Always use SVG for best quality

✓ Contrast Check

Ensure good contrast on backgrounds

❌ Don'ts

✗ Don't Stretch

Never compress or elongate the logo

✗ Change Colors

Don't modify the gold or black colors

✗ Add Gradients

No gradients beyond metallic foil effect

✗ Rotate/Flip

Never rotate or flip the logo

✗ Go Too Small

Never use below minimum size specs

✗ Low Contrast

Don't use on similar colored backgrounds
